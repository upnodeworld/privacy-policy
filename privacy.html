Privacy Policy for Price Twister (Chrome Extension)

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

What It Does

  • Modifies how prices appear on Amazon.com
  • Stores your preferences (conversion mode, custom units) using chrome.storage.sync
  • All data remains on your device — nothing is sent to any server

Data Collection

No analytics, tracking, or cookies are used.
